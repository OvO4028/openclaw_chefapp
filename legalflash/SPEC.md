# LegalFlash - Product Specification

## Vision
AI-powered legal document generator that creates GDPR/CCPA compliant Privacy Policies, Terms of Service, and Cookie Policies in seconds - saving users €500+ vs lawyers.

## Target Users
- Solo entrepreneurs
- Small businesses
- Freelancers
- Startups
- Bloggers/content creators

## Core Features

### 1. Privacy Policy Generator
- GDPR & CCPA compliant
- Customizable based on business type
- Data collection disclosure
- User rights sections

### 2. Terms of Service Generator
- Comprehensive ToS protection
- Limitation of liability
- User conduct rules
- Termination clauses

### 3. Cookie Policy Generator
- GDPR-compliant
- Cookie categorization
- Consent management guidance

## Pricing

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Free** | €0 | 1 document |
| **Pro** | €19/mo | Unlimited, all types, auto-updates |
| **Business** | €49/mo | Priority + lawyer review |
