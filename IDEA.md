# Business Idea: "LegalFlash" - AI Legal Document Generator

## The Problem
- Every website/app needs legal docs (Privacy Policy, Terms, Cookie Policy)
- Lawyers cost €500-2000+
- Templates are generic and often invalid
- Small businesses can't afford proper legal docs

## The Solution
**LegalFlash** - Instant AI-generated legal documents

## Features
1. **Privacy Policy Generator** - GDPR, CCPA compliant
2. **Terms of Service** - Website/app/ecommerce
3. **Cookie Policy** - GDPR ePrivacy Directive
4. **Disclaimer Generator** - Affiliate/disclaimer content
5. **Data Processing Agreement** - For contractors/vendors

## How It Works
1. Select document type
2. Answer 5-10 questions (company name, what data you collect, countries you serve, etc.)
3. Download ready-to-use legal document
4. Optional: Lawyer review add-on (€49)

## Pricing
- **Free:** 1 basic document
- **Pro (€19/mo):** Unlimited documents, all types, updates
- **Business (€49/mo):** Custom branding, lawyer review included

## Why This Wins
- Massive market (every website needs these)
- Clear ROI (€19 vs €500+ lawyer)
- Highly scalable (AI生成 = near-zero marginal cost)
- Recurring revenue (laws change, docs need updates)
- Trust is high-stakes = people pay

## Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Streamlit (free)
- **Hosting:** Streamlit Cloud (free)
- **AI:** OpenAI GPT-4 (€0.01-0.03/doc)
- **Payments:** Stripe
